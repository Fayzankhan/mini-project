Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the pneumonia detection?
Our model has been trained on a large dataset of chest X-rays and achieves an accuracy of over 95% in detecting pneumonia. However, it's important to note that this is an AI-assisted tool and should be used in conjunction with professional medical expertise.
What types of X-ray images are supported?
We support standard chest X-ray images in common formats like JPG, PNG, and DICOM. The images should be clear and properly centered on the chest area.
How is the confidence score calculated?
The confidence score is calculated based on multiple factors including model accuracy, image quality assessment, feature detection, and pattern recognition. Each factor contributes to the overall confidence level.
